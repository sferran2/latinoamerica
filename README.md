Knowledge about the world has become much more accessible to everyone thanks to the internet and its vast, immeasurable sources of information. 
This year, my husband is celebrating five years at the same company and was given a sabbatical month. We decided to travel through Latin America, 
and in the process of planning, I visited many websites to gather information. That experience inspired me to create an app focused specifically 
on Latinoamerica app that highlights each country’s most relevant facts, places to visit, suggested travel durations, and even the level of 
difficulty for different travelers (such as those with young children, older adults, or travelers with specific needs). My goal is to promote tourism,
inform users, and maybe even help them discover something new about Latin America, whether they're planning a trip or simply want to learn more.


The LATINOAMERICA Learn and Go App, with a focus on Latin America, is designed for curious learners, travelers, and families planning a trip to the region.
It’s especially helpful for those who want trustworthy, quick information without digging through multiple sources. Whether it’s a student doing a geography
project, a couple planning a sabbatical adventure, or a parent looking for kid-friendly destinations, this app provides essential facts, inspiring images, and
practical travel tips. By including features like travel difficulty level and accessibility, the app supports a wide variety of users—from solo travelers to 
families with young children, and even older adults looking for suitable destinations.
